Super-Bill Submission

Clean, complete super-bills submitted accurately — maximizing first-pass claim acceptance

WHAT IS A SUPER-BILL

The Foundation of Every Successful Claim

Our coders collaborate with the billing team to generate a super-bill that details charges, patient insurance coverage, diagnosis codes, procedure codes, and any co-payments. This comprehensive document is the basis for every insurance claim submitted.

A properly completed super-bill ensures that every service is captured, correctly coded, and submitted with all required documentation — dramatically reducing rejections and denials.

WHAT'S INCLUDED

Complete Super-Bill Components

  • Patient demographics and insurance information
  • Provider NPI and taxonomy codes
  • Date of service and place of service codes
  • ICD-10 diagnosis codes (primary + secondary)
  • CPT/HCPCS procedure codes
  • Modifier codes where applicable
  • Units and charges per service line
  • Co-pay and patient responsibility amounts
  • Referring provider information if required
  • Prior authorization numbers
