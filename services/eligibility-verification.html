Eligibility Verification

Verify patient insurance before every visit — eliminating claim denials at the source

PREVENT DENIALS

The First Line of Defense Against Claim Denials

Verifying patient insurance eligibility is the crucial first step in billing, requiring precise and timely collection of all necessary information. When eligibility isn't verified upfront, practices risk treating patients with lapsed coverage, wrong plans, or coverage limitations — leading to costly denials.

Our team verifies every patient's coverage 24–48 hours before their appointment, giving your front desk the exact information they need to collect co-pays, deductibles, and notify patients of coverage limitations.

VERIFICATION SCOPE

Everything Checked Before Every Visit

Active Coverage

Confirm the patient's plan is active and current at time of service.

Benefits & Deductibles

Identify remaining deductible and out-of-pocket amounts for each patient.

Co-pay Amounts

Confirm exact co-pay due at time of service to eliminate billing surprises.

Authorization Requirements

Flag all services requiring prior authorization before the appointment.

Network Status

Confirm provider is in-network for the patient's current insurance plan.

Coordination of Benefits

Identify primary vs. secondary coverage for accurate claim sequencing.
