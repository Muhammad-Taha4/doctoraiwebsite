Credentialing & Enrollment

Get credentialed faster with all major payers — so you can see patients and get paid without delays

CREDENTIALING EXPERTS

Don't Let Credentialing Delays Cost You Revenue

Medical credentialing is the process by which insurance companies verify a physician's qualifications, licensure, and practice information before allowing them to participate in their network. Without active credentials, you cannot bill payers for your services — costing your practice thousands daily.

Our specialists manage the entire credentialing and re-credentialing process from start to finish — from primary source verification to contract negotiations to CAQH profile maintenance.
