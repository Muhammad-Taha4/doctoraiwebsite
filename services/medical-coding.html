WHY IT MATTERS

Why Accurate Coding Matters

Under-Coding Costs You Money

Selecting lower codes than warranted means leaving money on the table. The average practice loses $15K–$40K annually to under-coding — revenue that was rightfully earned.

Over-Coding Triggers Audits

Upcoding triggers payer audits, recoupments, and significant penalties. Our certified coders ensure you're always billing at the correct, defensible level.

Our Solution: Always Right, Always Defensible

Our certified coders ensure every claim is coded accurately, completely, and in compliance with all payer guidelines — maximizing revenue without risk.
